<p><strong>The Single-Ended Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Single-Ended Probes</li><li>Active Single-Ended Probes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single-Ended Probe market consists of two main types: passive and active single-ended probes. Passive single-ended probes are simple, low-cost devices that rely on a resistive-divider network for signal measurement, making them suitable for lower frequency applications. In contrast, active single-ended probes incorporate amplification and buffering, allowing for higher frequency measurements with reduced loading effects. Both probe types are essential in electronics testing, providing versatility in applications ranging from research and development to validation and debugging in various industries.</p></p>
