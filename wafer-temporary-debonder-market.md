<p><p>The Wafer Temporary Debonder market is segmented into several types, including Thermal Debond, Mechanical Debond, Laser Debond, and Others. Thermal Debond utilizes heat to separate wafers from their carriers, often enhancing yield. Mechanical Debond relies on physical methods, such as adhesive tape, to detach wafers. Laser Debond employs laser energy for precise debonding, offering high accuracy and flexibility. The "Others" category includes emerging or specialized techniques not classified in the primary types, reflecting ongoing innovation in the market.</p></p>
