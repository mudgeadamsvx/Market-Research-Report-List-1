The market is divided into two main types: non-carded and carded thermoformed skin packaging. Non-carded thermoforming involves directly sealing the product without a backing card, offering a lightweight solution for items like fresh meats. In contrast, carded thermoformed skin packaging includes a backing card, providing additional support and branding opportunities, commonly used for consumer goods such as electronics or tools.</p></p>
