<p><p>The polyester fiber market consists primarily of two segments: Polyester Staple Fiber (PSF) and Polyester Filament Yarn (PFY). PSF is short, staple-length fibers used in textiles, non-woven fabrics, and filling materials, valued for durability and cost-effectiveness. PFY, on the other hand, consists of continuous strands suitable for high-performance applications like apparel and industrial textiles, offering smoothness and high tensile strength. Together, these fibers cater to diverse industries, enhancing versatility and driving market growth.</p></p>
