<p><strong>The Escalator Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Step Chains</li><li>Drive Chains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The escalator chain market encompasses various types of chains used in escalators and moving walkways, primarily categorized into step chains and drive chains. Step chains are responsible for supporting and moving escalator steps, ensuring smooth operation and passenger safety. Drive chains, on the other hand, transmit power from the motor to the escalator mechanism, facilitating the movement of steps. Both chain types are essential for efficient escalator function, impacting maintenance, longevity, and overall performance in public and commercial spaces.</p></p>
