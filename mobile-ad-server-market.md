<p><strong>The Mobile Ad Server Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>First Party and Third Party Ad Servers</li><li>Hosted and Self-Hosted Ad Servers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile ad server market can be segmented into two main types: first-party and third-party ad servers. First-party ad servers manage and deliver ads directly from a brand or publisher, allowing for better control over data and user targeting. In contrast, third-party ad servers serve ads from external advertisers, providing a wider reach but less data ownership. Additionally, ad servers can be hosted or self-hosted; hosted solutions offer cloud-based management, while self-hosted servers give companies full control over their infrastructure and data.</p></p>
