<p><p>The semiconductor test socket market encompasses various types, including BGA (Ball Grid Array), QFP (Quad Flat Package), QFN (Quad Flat No-lead), LGA (Land Grid Array), and SOIC (Small Outline Integrated Circuit). Each type serves distinct semiconductor packaging needs, facilitating effective testing and assembly processes. BGA sockets are ideal for high pin-count devices, while QFP and QFN are used for surface mount technologies. LGA sockets provide reliable connections for high-performance applications, and SOIC is popular for compact designs. Other variations cater to specialized testing requirements.</p></p>
