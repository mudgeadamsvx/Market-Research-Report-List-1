<p><p>Single-phase gear motors are essential components in various applications, divided primarily into solid-shaft and hollow-shaft types. Solid-shaft gear motors feature a continuous shaft, providing reliable torque transmission and straightforward integration into machinery. In contrast, hollow-shaft gear motors have a cylindrical inner core that allows for easier mounting and alignment, accommodating different shaft sizes. Both types serve diverse industries, from automation to appliances, emphasizing efficiency and versatility in powering systems and equipment.</p></p>
