<p><p>The electrically active smart glass market comprises various types with distinct functionalities. Suspended Particle Device (SPD) glass utilizes suspended particles to control light transmission. Electrochromic glass changes tint with electrical current, enhancing energy efficiency. Liquid Crystal/Polymer Disperse Liquid Crystal glass adjusts transparency using electric signals. Micro-blinds feature tiny movable slats for light management. Nanocrystal glass employs nanotechnology to switch between opaque and transparent states, offering versatile privacy and energy-saving solutions for buildings and vehicles.</p></p>
