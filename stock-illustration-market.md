<p><p>Stock illustration markets are categorized into three main types: macrostock, midstock, and microstock. Macrostock platforms offer high-quality, exclusive images often priced at a premium; they cater to professional clients needing unique visuals. Midstock bridges the gap, providing quality images at moderate prices, appealing to small businesses and freelancers. Microstock allows for lower-cost images, making it accessible to a broader audience, often featuring diverse collections with lower quality and royalty rates, suitable for personal or non-commercial use.</p></p>
