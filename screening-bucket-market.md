<p><strong>The Screening Bucket Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Star Screening Bucket</li><li>Rotary Screening Bucket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screening buckets are specialized attachments for excavators, designed to sift and separate materials on construction and demolition sites. The star screening bucket utilizes rotating stars to effectively separate larger debris from finer materials, making it ideal for compost, soil, and waste processing. In contrast, the rotary screening bucket employs a rotating drum with mesh or perforated surfaces to classify various aggregate sizes. Both types enhance efficiency in material handling, catering to diverse project needs in the construction and recycling sectors.</p></p>
