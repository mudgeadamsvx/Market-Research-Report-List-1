<p><p>Shrink fit machines are used in various industries to assemble components securely through thermal expansion. The market can be divided into two main types: dry heat styling and moist heat styling. </p><p>Dry heat styling employs heated air or surfaces to expand the components, allowing for easy fit and secure assembly. In contrast, moist heat styling utilizes steam or moisture to create a softer interface, facilitating a more gentle fit without excessive thermal stress, making it suitable for delicate materials.</p></p>
