<p><p>The kidney transplant surgery instrument market comprises various specialized tools. Tissue forceps are used for grasping and manipulating tissues during surgery. Atraumatic vascular clamps minimize damage to blood vessels while providing secure occlusion. Buffer vein retractors help maintain visibility and access to surgical sites by gently holding back tissues. Bulldog clamps are essential for temporarily occluding blood vessels during the procedure. Other instruments may include needle holders, scissors, and suction devices, all designed to enhance surgical efficiency and safety.</p></p>
