<p><p>The absorption cooling device market is primarily segmented into two types: Double Absorption Refrigerating Devices and Three Effect Absorption Refrigerating Devices. Double absorption employs two stages of heat recovery, enhancing efficiency and is suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, three-effect systems utilize a more complex design, maximizing thermal efficiency by sequentially utilizing heat in multiple stages, making them ideal for larger industrial operations. Both systems leverage heat sources for cooling, promoting sustainable energy use.</p></p>
