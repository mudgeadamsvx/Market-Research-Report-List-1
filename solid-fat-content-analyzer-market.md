<p><p>The Solid Fat Content (SFC) Analyzer market is segmented into two primary types based on measurement methods: the direct method and the indirect method. The direct method involves evaluating solid fat content through techniques such as nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR) or X-ray diffraction, providing accurate and immediate results. In contrast, the indirect method typically relies on correlations with other parameters, like viscosity or melting point, offering a quicker but potentially less precise assessment. Both methods cater to various industry needs in fat analysis.</p></p>
