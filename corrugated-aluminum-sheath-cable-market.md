<p><p>The Corrugated Aluminum Sheath Cable market consists of two primary types: High Voltage (HV) Corrugated Aluminum Sheath Cable and Extra High Voltage (EHV) Corrugated Aluminum Sheath Cable. HV cables are designed for applications operating at voltages ranging from 1 kV to 35 kV, providing reliable conductivity and protection. EHV cables, on the other hand, are intended for voltage ratings above 35 kV, enabling efficient transmission over long distances while minimizing energy losses. Both types are crucial for modern electrical infrastructure.</p></p>
