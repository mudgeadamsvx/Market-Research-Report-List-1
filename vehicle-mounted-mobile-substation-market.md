<p><p>The vehicle-mounted mobile substation market encompasses three key types: HV, LV, and MV mobile substations. HV mobile substations operate at high voltage levels, typically used for transmitting large amounts of electricity over long distances, ensuring grid stability. LV mobile substations cater to low voltage requirements, often deployed for localized distribution and emergency situations. MV mobile substations bridge the gap between high and low voltage applications, suitable for regional distribution needs, making all three crucial for efficient energy management and rapid deployment during outages or expansions.</p></p>
