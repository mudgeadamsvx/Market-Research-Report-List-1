<p><p>The Intelligent Power Distribution Unit (PDU) market is categorized into two main types: Single Phase and Three Phase units. Single Phase PDUs are designed for lower power requirements, commonly used in smaller data centers and office environments, providing energy distribution for servers and networking equipment. In contrast, Three Phase PDUs cater to larger facilities with high power demands, enabling efficient distribution across multiple devices and ensuring better load balancing and performance in critical power applications, particularly in enterprise data centers.</p></p>
