<p><strong>The Trenchless Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Directional Drilling Machine</li><li>Pipe Jacking Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The trenchless equipment market includes various technologies for underground construction with minimal surface disruption. Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) machines facilitate the installation of pipelines and utilities by drilling horizontally beneath the ground, ideal for long-distance projects. Pipe Jacking machines, on the other hand, involve pushing pipes through the ground using hydraulic jacks, suitable for installing sewer, water, and stormwater lines in urban environments. Both methods enhance efficiency and reduce environmental impact compared to traditional excavation techniques.</p></p>
