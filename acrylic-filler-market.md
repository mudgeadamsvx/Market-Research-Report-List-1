<p><p>The acrylic filler market comprises two main types: one-component and two-component acrylic fillers. One-component acrylic fillers are ready-to-use products that require no mixing, offering convenience and easier application for users. In contrast, two-component acrylic fillers necessitate the combination of a resin and a hardener before use, resulting in enhanced durability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for more demanding applications. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, construction, and woodworking, addressing specific repair and finishing needs.</p></p>
