<p><p>The parcel conveyor system market comprises various types, notably roller conveyors and belt conveyors. Roller conveyors utilize a series of rollers to facilitate the movement of parcels, allowing for efficient gravity or powered flow, ideal for heavy or bulky items. Belt conveyors, on the other hand, employ a continuous belt to transport parcels, offering flexibility and smooth handling of various package sizes. Both systems enhance operational efficiency and are crucial in logistics, warehousing, and distribution centers.</p></p>
