<p><ul><li>Seamless</li><li>Welded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel precision tubes market is categorized into seamless and welded types. Seamless tubes are manufactured without joints, offering superior strength and uniformity, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Conversely, welded tubes are formed by joining flat stainless steel strips, providing cost-effectiveness and versatility for varying thicknesses and sizes. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and construction, with seamless tubes often preferred for demanding environments, while welded tubes serve broader applications.</p></p>
