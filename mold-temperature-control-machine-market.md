<p><p>The Mold Temperature Control Machine market is primarily divided into Water Type and Oil Type Temperature Controllers. Water Type Temperature Controllers utilize water as the heat transfer medium, offering efficient cooling and heating, making them ideal for precise temperature control in injection molding processes. In contrast, Oil Type Temperature Controllers use thermal oil, providing higher temperature ranges and superior heat retention. Both types cater to various industrial applications, ensuring optimal mold performance and enhanced product quality in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
