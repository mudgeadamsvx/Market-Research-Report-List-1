<p><p>The Stretch Blow Moulders market is primarily categorized into two types: Rotary Stretch Blow Moulders and Linear Stretch Blow Moulders. Rotary Stretch Blow Moulders utilize a rotating platform to enhance production efficiency and increase output, making them ideal for high-volume applications. In contrast, Linear Stretch Blow Moulders operate in a straight line, offering flexibility in production and a simpler design, suitable for smaller-scale operations. Both types are essential for producing PET bottles and containers in various industries, including beverages and personal care.</p></p>
