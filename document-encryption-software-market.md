<p><p>Document encryption software encompasses various algorithms enhancing data security. The IDEA (International Data Encryption Algorithm) ensures confidentiality through symmetric encryption with a 128-bit key. RSA (Rivest-Shamir-Adleman), a widely-used asymmetric algorithm, utilizes a pair of keys for secure data transmission. AES (Advanced Encryption Standard) is the most adopted symmetric encryption method, providing robust protection with 128, 192, and 256-bit keys. Each algorithm serves different needs, balancing speed and security for diverse applications in protecting sensitive information.</p></p>
