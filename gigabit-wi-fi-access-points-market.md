<p><p>The Gigabit Wi-Fi Access Points market is categorized into single-band and double-band types. Single-band access points operate on one frequency, typically 2.4 GHz, providing basic connectivity with lower performance and speed. In contrast, double-band access points support both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, offering enhanced bandwidth, reduced interference, and improved performance for multiple devices. Double-band access points cater to demanding applications, while single-band devices are suitable for simpler needs, allowing users to choose based on their requirements.</p></p>
