<p><p>The portable water purifier market includes various types designed for convenient access to clean water. Extrusion water purifiers use a filtration mechanism to remove impurities as water passes through. Pump water purifiers utilize a manual pump to push water through a filter system, ensuring effective purification. Suction water purifiers draw water through a filter via suction; ideal for limited access. UV pen purifiers employ ultraviolet light to kill harmful microorganisms, providing a chemical-free purification method for safe drinking water.</p></p>
