<p><strong>The Light Intensity Modulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Direct Modulation Type"</li><li>"Reflective Type"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light intensity modulator market is categorized into two main types: Direct Modulation Type and Reflective Type. </p><p>Direct Modulation Type involves varying the intensity of light directly from a source, typically using techniques like pulse-width modulation, enabling precise control for applications like communication systems. </p><p>Reflective Type, on the other hand, relies on altering light intensity through reflective surfaces or devices, often found in display technologies and optical sensors. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing signal quality and improving visual output across various industries.</p></p>
