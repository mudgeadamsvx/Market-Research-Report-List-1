<p><p>The automotive back-up camera market features three main types: surface mounted, flush mounted, and license mounted cameras. Surface mounted cameras are typically installed on the exterior, providing a wide field of view but can be more visible. Flush mounted cameras blend seamlessly into the vehicle's body for a discreet look while still offering effective monitoring. License mounted cameras are integrated into the vehicle's license plate area, providing a balance of visibility and aesthetics, commonly used in many modern vehicles for safety.</p></p>
