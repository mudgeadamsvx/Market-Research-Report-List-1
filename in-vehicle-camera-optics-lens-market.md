<p><p>The in-vehicle camera optics lens market includes two main types: visible lenses and infrared (IR) lenses. Visible lenses capture images in the visible light spectrum, essential for standard driving conditions and safety applications such as lane departure warnings and obstacle detection. In contrast, IR lenses are designed to operate in low-light or nighttime conditions, enhancing visibility by detecting heat signatures and providing clearer images when light is scarce, thus improving overall vehicle safety and functionality.</p></p>
