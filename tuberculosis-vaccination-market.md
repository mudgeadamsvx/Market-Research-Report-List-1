<p><p>The Tuberculosis vaccination market comprises two main types: immune vaccines and therapy vaccines. Immune vaccines, like the BCG vaccine, aim to enhance the body's immune response against tuberculosis by providing initial protection, especially in high-risk populations. Conversely, therapy vaccines are designed to boost immune responses in individuals already infected with TB, facilitating treatment and reducing disease severity. Both types are critical in the global fight against tuberculosis, targeting prevention and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
