<p><strong>The External Plug-In Adapters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC/AC</li><li>AC/DC</li><li>DC/DC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The external plug-in adapters market is categorized into three types: AC/AC, AC/DC, and DC/DC adapters. AC/AC adapters convert alternating current from the wall outlet into a different voltage of alternating current for devices like transformers. AC/DC adapters transform alternating current into direct current, commonly used for powering electronics like laptops and smartphones. DC/DC adapters are designed to adjust the voltage of an existing direct current supply, frequently used in automotive applications or to power circuit boards.</p></p>
