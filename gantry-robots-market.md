<p><p>The gantry robots market is divided into two main types: open and closed gantry robots. Open gantry robots feature an exposed structure, allowing for unobstructed access to the workspace, making them ideal for tasks requiring flexibility and quick tool changes. In contrast, closed gantry robots have a more enclosed design, providing enhanced stability and protection for sensitive components, making them suitable for precision applications in manufacturing environments. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing efficiency and automation.</p></p>
