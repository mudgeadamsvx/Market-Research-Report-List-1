<p><p>Thermoelectric Generator (TEG) modules are systems that convert heat directly into electricity using thermoelectric materials. The market is divided into two main types: single-stage modules, which utilize a single pair of thermoelectric legs to generate power from a temperature difference, and multistage modules, which employ multiple stages for enhanced efficiency by stacking several pairs of thermoelectric elements. Multistage modules are often preferred for high-temperature applications as they can achieve greater temperature differentials, leading to improved energy conversion.</p></p>
