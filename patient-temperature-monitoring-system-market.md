<p><ul><li>Non-invasive Temperature Monitoring</li><li>Invasive Temperature Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Patient Temperature Monitoring System market is divided into two main types: non-invasive and invasive temperature monitoring. Non-invasive monitoring methods, such as infrared thermometers and skin sensors, measure temperature without penetrating the body, offering quick and comfortable readings. In contrast, invasive temperature monitoring involves devices like thermocouples and rectal probes, which provide precise internal temperature measurements but may be less comfortable for the patient. Both types are essential for accurate patient assessment and management in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
