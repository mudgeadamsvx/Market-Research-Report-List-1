<p><ul><li>Water Media</li><li>Air Media</li><li>Electrical-Based Tubin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiant heating and cooling systems utilize various media for thermal exchange in buildings. Water media systems circulate heated or chilled water through pipes embedded in floors or ceilings, providing uniform temperature control. Air media systems rely on air movement, utilizing ductwork to distribute conditioned air. Electrical-based tubing systems, such as radiant electric floor heating, use electric cables or mats installed under flooring to generate heat directly. Each type offers distinct advantages in energy efficiency, comfort, and installation flexibility.</p></p>
