<p><strong>The Diesel Injection Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Distributor Pump</li><li>Individual Control Pump</li><li>Unit Injection</li><li>Common Rail System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diesel injection pumps market includes several key types: Rotary Distributor Pumps use a single pump to distribute fuel to multiple cylinders, ensuring efficiency. Individual Control Pumps provide precise fuel delivery to each cylinder independently, enhancing performance. Unit Injection systems integrate the injector with the pump, offering high pressure in compact designs. The Common Rail System maintains a single fuel rail supplying multiple injectors, allowing for better control of injection timing and pressure, thereby improving fuel efficiency and reducing emissions.</p></p>
