<p><p>The cold room market is primarily categorized into single chamber and multi-chamber cold rooms. Single chamber cold rooms are designed for uniform temperature control, ideal for storing a specific type of product, such as perishables or pharmaceuticals. In contrast, multi-chamber cold rooms feature separate compartments that allow for varied temperature settings, making them suitable for diverse products requiring different storage conditions. Both types enhance product preservation, reduce spoilage, and support various industries, including food, health, and logistics.</p></p>
