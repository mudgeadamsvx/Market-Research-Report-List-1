<p><p>The Semiconductor Optical Amplifier (SOA) market primarily includes Erbium Doped Fiber Amplifiers (EDFAs) and Linear Optical Amplifiers. EDFAs utilize erbium-doped fiber to amplify signals in optical networks, offering high gain and low noise, making them ideal for long-distance communication. Linear Optical Amplifiers, on the other hand, provide high-fidelity signal amplification with minimal distortion, catering to applications requiring precise signal replication. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing data transmission capabilities in telecommunications and data centers, driving market growth.</p></p>
