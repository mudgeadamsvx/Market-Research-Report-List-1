<p><ul><li>Electro-Pneumatic Single Acting Positioners</li><li>Electro-Pneumatic Double Acting Positioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electro-pneumatic positioners market is divided into two main types: single acting and double acting positioners. Single acting positioners utilize a spring return mechanism, allowing for quicker response under fail-safe conditions, making them suitable for applications requiring less exertion. In contrast, double acting positioners use pneumatic pressure to control the actuator in both directions, offering greater precision and control for demanding applications. These positioners play a crucial role in automation and process control across various industries.</p></p>
