<p><ul><li>Swing Rotor</li><li>Fixed Angle Rotor</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The universal centrifuge market is primarily categorized into three types: swing rotor, fixed angle rotor, and others. Swing rotor centrifuges allow for horizontal positioning of samples during rotation, providing efficient separation of particles by enabling a smoother sedimentation process. Fixed angle rotors hold samples at a constant angle, which enhances sedimentation speed and is ideal for quick separations. Other types may include specialized rotors designed for unique applications, such as microcentrifuge rotors or those for specific laboratory needs.</p></p>
