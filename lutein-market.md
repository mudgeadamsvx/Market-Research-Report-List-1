<p><p>The lutein market can be divided into two main types: free lutein and lutein esters. Free lutein is the unbound form, typically available in dietary supplements, known for its direct bioavailability and effectiveness in supporting eye health. In contrast, lutein esters are bound forms that require enzymatic conversion in the body, often used in fortified foods and beverages. Both types serve distinct purposes in nutrition and wellness, appealing to different consumer preferences in the eye care and dietary supplement sectors.</p></p>
