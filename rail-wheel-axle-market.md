<p><p>The Rail Wheel Axle market comprises primarily two types: rolled axles and forged axles. Rolled axles are produced by rolling steel into desired shapes, offering uniformity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for high-volume applications. In contrast, forged axles are created through the forging process, resulting in enhanced strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-load and high-stress situations. Both types cater to the rail industry's needs, balancing performance, safety, and cost considerations in rail vehicle manufacturing.</p></p>
