<p><strong>The Polyester Nonwoven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbond Method</li><li>Spunlace Method</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyester nonwoven fabric market is segmented into various types based on manufacturing methods. The **spunbond method** involves the continuous filament process, creating durable fabrics with high tensile strength, ideal for applications like hygiene products and geotextiles. The **spunlace method**, on the other hand, utilizes high-pressure water jets to entangle fibers, producing softer, more absorbent materials suitable for wipes and medical textiles. Additionally, there are **other methods** which include meltblown and needle-punching techniques, catering to diverse needs in various industries.</p></p>
