<p><ul><li>Sulfuric Acid Titanium Dioxide</li><li>Chlorination Titanium Dioxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Titanium Dioxide market is primarily categorized into two production methods: Sulfuric Acid Titanium Dioxide and Chlorination Titanium Dioxide. Sulfuric Acid Titanium Dioxide, produced through the sulfate process, is favored for its lower processing costs and environmental impact. In contrast, Chlorination Titanium Dioxide is obtained via the chloride process, offering superior purity and brightness, making it ideal for high-performance applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing pricing, demand, and application in sectors like coatings, plastics, and paper.</p></p>
