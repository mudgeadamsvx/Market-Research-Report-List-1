<p><strong>The GNSS Boards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Frequency</li><li>Multi-frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GNSS boards market is categorized into single-frequency and multi-frequency types. Single-frequency GNSS boards operate on a single frequency channel, primarily used for basic positioning applications, offering simplicity and lower costs. In contrast, multi-frequency GNSS boards utilize multiple frequency bands, enhancing accuracy and reliability in positioning by mitigating signal interference and multipath errors. This makes them suitable for advanced applications such as autonomous vehicles and precise geolocation services, driving demand for more sophisticated technology.</p></p>
