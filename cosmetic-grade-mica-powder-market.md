<p><p>Cosmetic grade mica powder is available in two primary forms: wet ground and dry ground. Wet ground mica is produced by dispersing mica in water, resulting in a finer, smoother particle size that enhances its application in cosmetics, providing a refined finish. Dry ground mica, on the other hand, is obtained through mechanical grinding without water, yielding a coarser texture. Both types are essential in cosmetics, offering varying benefits for color, shimmer, and coverage in products like eyeshadows and foundations.</p></p>
