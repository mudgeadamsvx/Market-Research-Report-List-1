<p><p>The electrolyte energy drink market can be divided into three main types: isotonic, hypotonic, and hypertonic drinks. Isotonic drinks have the same osmolarity as the bodyâ€™s fluids, providing rapid hydration and energy replenishment, suitable for intense exercise. Hypotonic drinks contain lower osmolarity, offering quicker hydration for light exercise or post-activity recovery. Hypertonic drinks are more concentrated, ideal for prolonged energy release, often consumed after intense workouts when carbohydrates are needed. Each type serves specific hydration and energy needs.</p></p>
